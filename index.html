Why YING

Two readings. One ambition.

  • Yonder
  • Infrastructure
  • Networks
  • Group

YING is read two ways — and we mean both.

We are an independent, boutique infrastructure advisory built on a simple idea: senior people, doing the actual work, held to an exacting standard. We help organisations make confident decisions about complex infrastructure — strategy, investment, procurement and delivery — across roads and the wider network of lifelines.

  • Senior-led delivery

    You work directly with experienced advisors — no hand-off to juniors.

  • Bilingual EN / 中文

    Genuine fluency that bridges NZ practice with Asia-Pacific partners.

  • Modern tools

    Modern analytical tools to test options and model scenarios — faster, deeper advice.

赢yíng · win 英yīng · excellence

赢 (yíng) — to win, to advance. The momentum to move a programme forward and secure the right outcome.

英 (yīng) — excellence, the British standard of engineering rigour. The discipline we hold every recommendation to.

Read together with Yonder — the long view, what lies beyond the build — they describe how we work.

What we do

Advisory across the infrastructure network

We advise four kinds of organisation — each with distinct pressures, each needing senior, independent counsel.

Public sector

Local Road Authorities (RCAs)

Strategy, asset management, and investment & procurement advice for road controlling authorities planning for the long term.

Contractors

Road Construction Companies

Commercial, procurement and delivery advisory — to bid sharper, price with confidence and deliver work profitably.

Consultancies

Engineering Consultants

Capability, bid / tender and programme advisory to strengthen your offer and win the right work.

3-Waters

3-Waters Asset Organisations

Asset planning and network & lifelines advisory for water, wastewater and stormwater design and construction.

Cross-cutting capabilities

  • Asset management
  • Procurement & tender strategy
  • Programme & project advisory
  • Infrastructure valuation
  • Network & lifelines planning

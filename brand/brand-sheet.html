01 — Logo system

The mark & lockups

An ascending “Y” lifts toward a horizon line — forward momentum (赢, to advance) meeting the long view (Yonder). A teal node marks the network hub. One master mark drives every variant.

Primary horizontal lockup — default for headers, letterheads, signatures.

Reversed — on navy, photography or dark backgrounds.

Stacked — square / centred placements.

Monogram — avatars, stamps, small spaces.

App icon / favicon — navy tile.

One-colour black — single-ink print, fax, engraving.

Clear space — keep free space on all sides equal to the height of the “Y” cap (shown dashed).

Minimum sizes

Monogram ≥ 24px

Favicon ≥ 16px

Full lockup ≥ 120px wide

04 — Usage

Do & don’t

  • Use the navy + teal lockup on light backgrounds.
  • Use the reversed (white) lockup on navy / photos.
  • Maintain clear space = the “Y” cap-height on all sides.
  • Keep teal for accents — links, CTAs, the node and horizon.
  • Outline the wordmark text for print / third-party handoff.
  • Don’t recolour the mark outside the palette.
  • Don’t stretch, rotate or add effects (shadows, bevels).
  • Don’t set teal body text on white (use Teal Ink, or navy).
  • Don’t place the navy mark on busy or low-contrast imagery.
  • Don’t add yin-yang, dragon or other literal motifs.
